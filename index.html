Understanding Doshas

In Ayurveda, doshas are the three energies that define every person's makeup. Learn about each dosha and its characteristics.

Vata Dosha

Composed of air and space elements, Vata governs movement and change in the body. When balanced, Vata promotes creativity and flexibility.

Creative Energetic Quick Adaptable Light

Pitta Dosha

Composed of fire and water elements, Pitta governs metabolism, digestion, and transformation. When balanced, Pitta promotes intelligence and courage.

Focused Intelligent Ambitious Precise Warm

Kapha Dosha

Composed of earth and water elements, Kapha governs structure, stability, and lubrication. When balanced, Kapha promotes strength and emotional stability.

Calm Nurturing Loyal Patient Strong

Prakriti Analysis

In Ayurveda, Prakriti is your innate physical and mental constitution. Knowing your Prakriti is the first step to adopting a healthy lifestyle.

Facial Features Reveal Your Prakriti

In Ayurveda, facial features are considered important indicators of a person's Prakriti (Dosha). Our advanced AI analyzes various facial features to determine your predominant doshas.

1

Feature Extraction

Utilize MobileNetV2 and ResNet50 for extracting features from facial images, capturing characteristics like face shape and skin texture.

2

Facial Landmark Detection

Apply MediaPipe to detect landmarks and compute geometric ratios, such as the face width-to-height ratio

3

Dosha Classification

What is Ayurveda?

Ayurveda is a traditional Indian system of medicine that is more than 5,000 years old. It focuses on creating harmony between body, mind, and spirit using diet, herbs, lifestyle practices, and various therapies. The word "Ayurveda" comes from Sanskrit, meaning "the science of life."

Can Ayurveda help with chronic conditions?

Ayurveda takes a holistic approach to health and can be beneficial for managing various chronic conditions by addressing root causes rather than just symptoms. However, it's important to note that Ayurveda should complement, not replace, conventional medical treatment. Always consult healthcare professionals when dealing with serious health conditions.
